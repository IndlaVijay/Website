The Best Dum Biryani in Town

What sets thebiryanibowl apart is the quality of its ingredients including all halal meats, antibiotic and hormone-free chicken, hand ground spices and fresh produce. We marinate all of our ingredients daily and never ever use pre­packaged foods or sauces. Our “Bhurani Ghol” are made from house made, organic yogurt and natural flavors.
